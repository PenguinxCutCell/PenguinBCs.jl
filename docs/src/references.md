2. S. C. Brenner and L. R. Scott, *The Mathematical Theory of Finite Element Methods*, 3rd ed., Springer, 2008.
3. A. Ern and J.-L. Guermond, *Theory and Practice of Finite Elements*, Springer, 2004.
4. R. Eymard, T. Gallouet, and R. Herbin, “Finite Volume Methods,” in *Handbook of Numerical Analysis*, Vol. 7, 2000.
